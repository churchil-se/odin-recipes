A very great way to prepare omelette

Ingredients

  1. Two Tomatoes
  2. One Onion
  3. A pinch of salt
  4. Royco Cube
  5. A little bit of love

Be very careful to use minimal amounts of salt

Make sure you have heated your pan with some oil in it. Then add onions and fry till opaque.
Next, add tomatoes and mash the combination till you have a smooth paste.
Lastly, pour in a beaten mix of eggs to the pan and toss and turn till the egg is cooked.
And that is how to prepare an omelette.
